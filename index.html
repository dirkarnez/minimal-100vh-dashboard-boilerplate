# Overview

# What is DAWG?

First and foremost, DAWG is a Digital Audio Workstation (DAW). It strives to provide all of the features expected out of a modern workstation while providing flexibility, extensibility, and new technology that leverages recent advances in machine learning.

# Should you use DAWG?

DAWG is in its early stages of development and is still missing a lot of small features that are present in DAWs such as FL Studio and Ableton. The features that do exist are not yet stable and may change at any time. Furthermore, DAWG still contains lots of hidden bugs that we have not found yet. If your adventurous and want to try something new, DAWG is for you.
